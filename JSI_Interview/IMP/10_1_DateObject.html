Date object

Date Object provides various methods to store and retrieve date and time information.

There are two different ways to measure the time, Global(World) time and Local Zone time.Global time is same for all throughout the world, whereas local time differs based on the timezone

Green Mean Time (GMT):

GMT measures time based on prime meridian line and divides the globe in time zones.
Prime Meridian is the 0 degree longitude line that divides the globe in half; it passes through GreenWich area, London.

UTC(GMT) : Global/World's time standard

UTC(coordinated Universal time), formerly Known as GMT
UTC is a 24 hours time standard measures time precisely.
UTC+00:00 === GMT+00:00

IST : Local time standard (Indian Standard Time)

IST is used to observe date time throughout india, with a time offset of GMT+05:30
UTC+00:00 AM === GMT+00:00 AM === GMT+05:30 AM
