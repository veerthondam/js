Types of javascript Operators:

1.Logical operators : (&&, ||, !)

2.Arithmetic operators : (+,-, *,/,%)

3.Relational operators : (<,>,<=,>=,==,!=,===,!==)

4.Assignment operators : (=,SHA/AA:(+=,-=,*=,/=))

5.Bitwise operators : (&,|,~,^,<<,>>)

6.Increment & Decrement operators : (++,--)

7.Conditional operators : (?:)

8.Special operators : typeof, new, delete
